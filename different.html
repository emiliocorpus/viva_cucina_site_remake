These are classes where you will be doing the cooking and eating the results!

Unlike other cooking classes where the students just watch, take notes, cut a few herbs and stir a few liquids in a pot, Barbara’s classes are actual hands-on cooking classes. Students prepare the recipes from start to finish while talking out the process, so you can reproduce the recipes at home with ease.

Her classes are great for people who enjoy cooking and want to learn how to “cook outside the box”, however beginner cooks will still feel at ease cooking. Keeping things simple and planning is the key to successful entertaining!

Barbara has been cooking and entertaining for over 25 years and truly loves teaching others to cook and entertain effortlessly.
