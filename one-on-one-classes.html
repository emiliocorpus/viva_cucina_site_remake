One on one Classes With Culinary Instructor

Create your own culinary experience in our hands-on or demonstration class with the instructor. You select the cooking subject for your personalized class. You will prepare two recipes, in about three hours. Ask us about customizing your class to your cooking needs. The cost for this special class is $125 for one person, $240 for two people, $360 for three people and $450 for four people. This is a great way for you to take your time learning a new recipe or fine tuning a cooking skill. Many couples use this class as a fun way to have a night out together.
