Welcome to Viva Cucina!

«
»

The Bay Area’s Hands-on Cooking Classes, located in the Tri-Valley and San Ramon - where you “learn to cook with ease” with culinary instructor, Barbara Kelly, in a One-on-One Class or Private Group Class or join us for one of our Boutique Winemaker Dinner Events.

Barbara teaches her students the cooking process and takes the guest work out of each recipe, giving you the confidence and experience to become a better cook. In these hands-on cooking classes you get involved in preparing each step of the recipe and enjoy eating the results without having to do the dishes.

You will receive recipes, cooking tips and be able to ask detailed cooking questions while preparing your recipe. Viva Cucina takes care of the details; recipes, ingredients, shopping, instruction, kitchen setup and clean up.

To Sign Up for a Cooking Class feel free to contact us at vivacucina@yahoo.com or call at 925-364-7621.
